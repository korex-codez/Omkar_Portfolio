Department of General Science & Humanities

Department At A Glance:-

The department of General science& Humanitieswas established in the year 2009 with intake capacity of 360 students per year. This department is affiliated to A.I.C.T.E., NewDelhi,and M.S.B.T.EMumbai. General Science Department is back bone of technical education .This department offers different subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English& Communication Skills. The Department has well equipped class rooms with multimedia facilities and 3 well-furnished Labs,with all its essentials. The Department strives continually to excel the students in the field of communication skills and general science and apply the same for betterment of the human beings and ultimately the society.

Vision:

“ Strive to empower students with quality education in the field of Science, Mathematics & Humanities to lay solid foundation for Technical Education.”

Mission:

To enable students to effectively apply the domain knowledge to solve real world problems thus leveraging their potential for lifelong careers. To enable students to use scientific knowledge & humanistic skills for technological advancement based on values. To improve student’s excellence as per today’s technological era.
